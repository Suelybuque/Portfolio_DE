## Context

This engagement replaced a decade-old HDFS footprint that could no longer keep pace with IoT ingest rates or the latency expectations of modern BI. The mandate was clear: preserve historical data fidelity, introduce transactional guarantees, and give platform teams a path to self-serve without sacrificing governance.

## Architecture Decisions

We selected **Delta Lake on S3** as the system of record, **EMR on EKS** for elastic Spark compute, and **Terraform** for reproducible environment promotion. Unity-style catalog patterns were mirrored with AWS Glue + Lake Formation so domain teams could own tables without owning infrastructure.
